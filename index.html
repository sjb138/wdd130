Palawan

Palawan is an island on the western side of the Philippines Archipelago. A popular tourist destination full of white sand beaches, caves to explore and secret lagoons, perfect for a summer getaway! From the historical site of The Tabon Cave Complex, to the turtle sanctuary of San Vicente, or the ever famous El Nido, the island of Palawan has something to offer for everybody.
