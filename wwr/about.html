Our History

Founded in 1998, our rafting company began with a passion for adventure and the river. What started as a small family-run business has grown into one of the most trusted names in white-water rafting. With decades of experience and thousands of satisfied adventurers, we pride ourselves on creating unforgettable memories.

Adventure Awaits You!

We offer thrilling adventures, certified guides, and top-tier safety standards. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned paddler, our trips cater to all levels. Get ready for fun, adrenaline, and scenic beauty like no other!
